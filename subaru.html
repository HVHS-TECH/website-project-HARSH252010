Subaru WRX STI

Engine

The Subaru WRX STI is powered by a 2.5L turbocharged boxer engine (EJ257). This engine is unique becuase of its flat design, which helps lower the car's center of gravity and improves balance and handling. It produces strong power and is built for performance driving, especially in rally conditions where control and stabiltiy are important.

Features

The WRX STI comes with Subaru's famous Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system, which provides excellent grip on all types of roads, including rain, snow, and dirt. It also includes a sport-tuned suspension, agressive styling, large rear wing, and performance brakes. These features make it perfect racing and rally driving.

why Subaru?

Subaru is known for reliability, safety, and strong performance in tough conditions. The WRX STI is especially poplular in rally racing becuase of its all-whell drive system and durable engine.
